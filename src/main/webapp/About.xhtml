Osman Balci
is Director of Mobile/Cloud Software Engineering Lab and Professor of Computer Science (CS) at Virginia Tech. His current areas of expertise center on Apple mobile software engineering, cloud software engineering, digital game-based learning, and modeling and simulation. Dr. Balci is interested in creating solutions to complex problems by way of engineering mobile and cloud software applications. He received the Virginia Tech College of Engineering 2018 W.S. “Pete” White Innovation in Engineering Education Award for his creation of mobile and cloud software development courses in the CS curriculum and his hands-on “Learning by Examples” pedagogy.
